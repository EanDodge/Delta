Tutorial

--Game

You are a controlling a pirate on the seven sea in the year... well who knows anymore
His name is Greg and had huge plans for the future, the only thing is his dabloons

They were stolen away from him when the coast guard took his ship for having cannons
Now greg needs to get dabloons to upgrade his ship to fight enemies of the sea

Get dabloons by fighting enemies, and visiting different islands where you will find
puzzles that will give you a treasure map when you finish it

--Movement

To Move you will be using
w to move forward
s to move backwards
a to turn left
d to turn right
r to anchor down
