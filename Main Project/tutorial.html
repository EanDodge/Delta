Tutorial

--Game

You are a controlling a pirate on the seven seas in the year... well who knows anymore
His name is Greg and had huge plans to conquer the 5 seas, the only thing he needs is his doubloons

They were stolen away from him when the coast guard took his ship for having cannons
Now greg needs to get doubloons to upgrade his ship to the fight enemies of the sea

Get doubloons by fighting enemies, beating bosses, and visiting different islands where you will find
treasure chests that can be unlocked by solving puzzles.

--Movement

To Move you will be using
w to move your sails starboard side
s to move your sails port side
a to turn left
d to turn right
r to anchor down
p to enter DeLozier mode
