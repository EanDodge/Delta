Points of Sail

  • In this game the wind is always coming out of the North, so you can use this diagram for proper sail positioning.
  • The sail positions given show the most efficient sail positions for each angle of the boat.
  • As a rule of thumb, the sail should always be on the opposite side of where the wind is hitting your boat.
  • Also, the closer you get to the "No Sail Zone" the closer the sail should be to the middle of the boat.
